I am a professional photographer who specializes in abstract photos of nature. I have a niche for wanting to find the perfect photo. I have experience in long-range shots, close-ups, and at numerous events, from local to celebrity.

About Me

Hi, my name is Jack Joseph and I am a 29 year old man from the Denver, Colorado area. I come from a family of photographers, in which my mother and father loved to take me along for their professional photoshoots. I grew up in the small town Idaho Springs, Colorado, in which the beautiful scenery always garnered my attention. That is where my desire and drive to photograph really came from. I love looking for the perfect shots, even in more extreme ways than many photographers would ever dream of. I tend to go all out with my photography in order to get the best piece of art I could possibly get out of a situation. I am a graduate from Brigham Young University with a Master's in Commerical Photography. Here are some of my big accolades I have recieved as a photographer:
  • 1x Sony World Photography Award
  • 2x International Photography Awards(IPA)
  • Monocrome Awards International Black and White Photography Contest Winner
  • 1x World Press Photo Award Recipient
I hope that this is just the beginning of a long list of awards in the near future. I really value the end product of my work, and I know that you do too. Feel free to reach out if you never need a photographer. I would love to meet you and the uniqueness that comes along with it!
